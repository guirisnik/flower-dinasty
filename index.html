When skies feel dim and heavy gray,

And shadows stretch across your day,

Remember there’s a light inside,

A warmth that time cannot divide.

Though now it seems the clouds won't part,

Hope still beats within your heart.

Take gentle steps and breathe in deep,

For brighter days are yours to keep.

I hope you find your spark anew,

And soon you'll smile, as you do.

This little card, with love, I send—

To let you know, you’ll rise again.
